The Art Of: CoalFather

Coalfather Industries represents a shared vision between artists Kara Jansson and Craig Newsom. The character of The Coalfather–developed during an online chat–infuses and informs their collaboration. The Coalfather is a once sleeping demon aroused into a New Morning by the subconscious chorus of a civilization hellbent on consumption, convenience and corn syrup. It is this ribald and roguish spirit of The Coalfather that becomes a lens through which our world is viewed.

CoalFather goes on answering The 5 Nimble Q's :
